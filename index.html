International Motor Racing Research Center

Town: Downtown Watkins GlenDrive: ~15 minCost: FreeHours: Mon–Fri 9–5

What: Free racing archive & museum — historic photos, memorabilia, model cars, and a short film on Watkins Glen’s racing roots.

Pair with: The historic village street circuit (below) — the center is steps from where the 1948 course started.

Phone: (607) 535-9044

ℹ️ Visit info🚗 Directions

Historic Grand Prix Circuit

Type: Self-drive tourTown: Village streetsCost: FreeKids: 5/5

What: Follow marked signs on public roads through downtown Watkins Glen — the original 6.6-mile course from 1948, when races ran past the post office and over train tracks.

Tip: Easy add-on before/after IMRRC or lunch on Tuesday. No ticket needed — just drive the route at normal speed.
